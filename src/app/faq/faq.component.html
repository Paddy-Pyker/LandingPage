Why was Kaneadua made?

Kaneadua was made to give game developers a marketplace where they get to control how their content is sold online. Kaneadua is an Internet-based digital distribution platform of games and programs for independent game creators and companies.


I'm a game developer / musician / comic artist / writer!

How do I discover cool stuffs on Kaneadua?How do I discover cool stuffs on Kaneadua?

Here’s a few tips to discover cool new content on kaneadua.com:
• Keep an eye on the front page – the projects featured there meet a certain level of quality and originality.
• Follow the people that interest you. Don’t have anyone in mind? check out the global creator feed or simply keep an eye on the new releases
• If you have an account, browse through my recommendations, which are tailored to the content you've played or downloaded. Choicely, Click the View related games button on any project page to find more of the same!


How can I keep up with my favorite creators?

• You can follow any creator, and it'll appear in your personalized feed. There you'll find cool stuffs like updates, ratings, new projects, new uploads, etc.


I've lost my purchase confirmation e-mail! How do I get the link again?

You can recover all purchases made with a given e-mail by filling the form on our support page.
Or else, you can access all your purchases by logging into your kaneadua account and navigating to My purchases or My collections.
Registering for kaneadua with an e-mail already used for purchase will automatically link all your previous purchases with your new account as soon as you verify the email address on the account.


Found a(n) Offensive / Copyright-infringing/ Broken page. What do I do?

Click on the Report link. It will open a form that sends a message directly to the kaneadua.com team, which the team will take appropriate action.
